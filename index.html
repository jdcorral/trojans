Welcome....Yes, it's finally here. This is the first iteration of my long anticipated website, and I mean long! The contents spans many years of drawing, designing, walking racetracks and airshow fields for hours, braving nature and just being lucky. As I remember things that I may have forgotten to put in this version, I will add them. It's a work in progress and will need updating often. If you have any comments, please feel free to shoot me an email. That said, I hope you find something of interest somewhere in all this.

Thanks for visiting.
